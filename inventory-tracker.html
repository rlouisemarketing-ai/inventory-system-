Stock · Profit · Restock — all in one place
1
Navigate your inventory
On mobile, use the bottom tabs (Dashboard, Products, Stock, Profit). On desktop, use the left sidebar.
2
Add your products
Go to Products → tap "+ Add Product". Choose Simple Mode for basic tracking or Advanced Mode for material, labor & fee breakdowns.
3
Track stock movements
Use the Stock tab to log sales, arrivals, returns, and adjustments. Quantities update automatically.
4
Back up your data
Your data is stored only on this device. Tap "💾 Save Backup Now" on the Dashboard regularly to protect it.
